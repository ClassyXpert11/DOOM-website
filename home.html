Doom Eternal is a sequel to the 2016 Doom reboot. The game launched a day early on March 19, 2020, for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, PC, and Google Stadia. The Nintendo Switch version was delayed and was released on December 8, 2020. The PlayStation 5 version was released on June 29, 2021. The Xbox X/S version was released on the Xbox Gamepass. It was followed up by The Ancient Gods - Part One on October 20th, 2020. A sequel to this, The Ancient Gods - Part Two, was released on March 18th, 2021.
